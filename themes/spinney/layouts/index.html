By Car

Get off the M40 at J15 and take the A46 towards Coventry. Leave the A46 at the Stoneleigh junction and take the Stoneleigh road towards Stoneleigh. After passing through Stonleigh and crossing the bridge, turn right onto the Coventry Road. Follow this road for a mile until you reach a junction where you should turn left onto the A445. After about 500 meters you will reach a junction to Weston Lane on the right. Turn right at this junction and after a while you will find the Spinney on the left.

By Train

Our nearest train stations are Leamington Spa and Coventry.
